Power Management¶

All power-managment functionality is handled by the TI-RTOS power driver and are used by the peripheral drivers (e.g. UART, SPI, I2C, etc..). Applications can prevent, if they choose, the CC2640R2 from entering low power modes by setting a power constraint.

More information on power-management functionality, see Power Management User’s Guide. These APIs are required only when using a custom driver.
